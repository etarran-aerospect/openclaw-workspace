- Ethan asked how I can help analyze multispectral and thermal vineyard imagery in QGIS. I replied with a practical workflow: align rasters, calculate NDVI/NDRE/GNDVI/OSAVI, normalize thermal to canopy/flight context, mask to vine canopy, run zonal stats by blocks/rows/irrigation zones, and generate stress zones plus scouting CSVs.
