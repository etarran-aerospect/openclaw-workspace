Year-Round Value: Seasonal Aerial Intelligence

AeroSpect provides critical, actionable insights tailored to each unique phase of your vineyard's annual cycle, ensuring optimal management from dormancy through harvest.

Winter (Dec – Feb)

Focus: Pruning, Tying & Site Preparation

Actionable Insights:

  • Structural Mapping: High-resolution RGB imagery precisely maps trellis systems and identifies broken posts or wires, guiding efficient winter repairs and pruning strategies.
  • Cover Crop Vigor: Multispectral analysis monitors cover crop establishment and vigor, providing insights for maintaining soil health during dormancy.
  • Drainage Analysis: Thermal imagery identifies drainage issues or soil moisture pooling, critical for planning erosion control or regrading.

Spring (Mar – May)

Focus: Canopy Management, Nutrients & Frost Protection

Actionable Insights:

  • Vigor & Nutrient Zoning: Multispectral imagery provides early-season vigor mapping, identifying hot spots or weak zones to guide precise nutrient application strategies.
  • Frost Risk & Efficacy: Thermal monitoring tracks temperature inversions and assesses the real-time efficiency of wind fans or sprinklers for effective frost protection.
  • Pest & Disease Scouting: High-resolution RGB maps detail early pest and disease outbreaks, enabling targeted and reduced-input spray programs.

Summer (Jun – Aug)

Focus: Irrigation, Veraison & Canopy Thinning

Actionable Insights:

  • Water Stress Detection: Thermal imagery accurately detects water stress in individual vines *before* visible wilting, optimizing irrigation schedules and water conservation.
  • Veraison Uniformity: Multispectral analysis tracks the uniformity of veraison, monitoring leaf color and canopy health to identify sections ripening at different rates.
  • Canopy Management Guidance: RGB vigor maps highlight areas requiring targeted leaf removal or thinning to optimize sunlight exposure and airflow.

Fall (Sep – Nov)

Focus: Harvest & Erosion Control

Actionable Insights:

  • Selective Harvest Maps: Combined Multispectral & RGB data create precise maps, identifying areas with consistent sugar/acid levels for optimal, quality-driven picking.
  • Soil Stability for Harvest: Thermal imagery monitors soil moisture to ensure ground stability for heavy harvest equipment, preventing compaction and facilitating smooth operations.
  • Post-Harvest Planning: High-resolution RGB maps aid in planning erosion control and cover crop seeding, setting the stage for a productive upcoming winter.
