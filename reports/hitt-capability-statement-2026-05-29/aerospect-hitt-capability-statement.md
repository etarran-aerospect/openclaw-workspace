# AeroSpect Inc.

## Aerial Reality Capture, GIS Mapping & Site Intelligence for Mission Critical Construction

Prepared for HITT Contracting

**Primary fit:** Mission Critical, industrial, energy, construction technology, and large-site civil work.

### Company Snapshot

AeroSpect Inc. provides professional drone-based aerial data capture, photogrammetry, GIS mapping, thermal imaging, and construction progress documentation. The company is led by a former commercial general contractor and former government contractor for security installations on military bases, with more than 3,000 licensed drone flight hours across construction, energy, agriculture, and infrastructure environments.

AeroSpect is not a generic drone photo vendor. We understand jobsite sequencing, access control, site logistics, owner documentation, and the value of repeatable field intelligence for project managers, VDC teams, owner representatives, and trade partners.

### Core Services

- Recurring aerial construction progress documentation
- High-resolution existing-condition photo records
- Orthomosaic mapping and photogrammetry deliverables
- GIS-ready site layers, annotations, and map packages
- Site logistics, laydown, access road, drainage, and utility corridor documentation
- Thermal imaging support for roofs, electrical infrastructure, equipment, and site heat concerns
- Perimeter and security infrastructure documentation: fencing, gates, access routes, camera corridors, and potential blind spots
- Stakeholder-ready reporting for PMs, owner reps, consultants, and field teams

### Mission Critical / Data Center Applications

- Pre-construction site baseline documentation
- Weekly or monthly progress flights for large campus-style builds
- Earthwork, grading, trenching, and utility corridor documentation
- Visual status records for subcontractor coordination and owner reporting
- GIS-based tracking of site access, circulation, perimeter controls, and phased work areas
- Thermal and multispectral support where specialty sensing adds value
- Rapid independent documentation after weather, site events, disputes, or schedule impacts

### Differentiators

- Former commercial GC perspective: understands construction workflow, documentation needs, and field constraints.
- Military-base security installation background: relevant to perimeter, access control, sensitive sites, and critical infrastructure environments.
- 3,000+ licensed drone flight hours: experienced in complex operating environments, not just basic real estate capture.
- GIS analyst capability: deliverables can become organized site intelligence, not just imagery folders.
- Thermal and multispectral specialization: useful for infrastructure, energy, roof, vegetation, water, and heat-related analysis.
- Established Northern California operator: available for recurring regional project support while maintaining specialty vineyard and agricultural work.
