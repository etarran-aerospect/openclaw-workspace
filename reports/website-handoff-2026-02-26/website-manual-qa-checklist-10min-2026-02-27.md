## 2) NDVI Section (3 min)
- [ ] Left NDVI / right RGB alignment looks correct

## 3) Accessibility Basics (2 min)
- [ ] Keyboard tab order is logical
- [ ] Interactive elements have visible focus states
- [ ] Images have alt text
- [ ] Heading order is sane (single H1, then H2/H3)

## 4) Conversion + Contact Path (1 min)
- [ ] Primary CTA text is clear and consistent
- [ ] CTA buttons route to expected target
- [ ] Contact form / contact method is reachable and usable

## 5) Performance Quick Check (1 min)
- [ ] Images feel fast enough on first load
- [ ] NDVI images are compressed (target <450KB each)
- [ ] No obvious jank during scroll/interactions
